About Deft

At Deft Consulting, we strive to provide the best in IT engineering services. Focusing on engineering excellence, we help to create a strong foundation on which to run your digital business.

With engineering services covering network, system, and cloud, we have the breadth of knowledge to make your projects successful. Founded in 2016, Deft Consulting may seem young, but our engineering experience is not. Our team has many years of large enterprise experience in various disciplines. We feel that our value lies in our engineering talent and we strive to employ only the best engineers.
